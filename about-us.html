Event

Event

TECHNOVACIÒN'22 is a 3 Day International level virtual Hackathon conducted by Sri Sairam Engineering College IEEE Computer Society sponsored by IEEE SPAA in Collaboration with IEEE Educational Society of Sri Sairam Engineering College on, the 4th, 5th, and 6th of May 2022.

The motto of this event is to encourage the student community to transform their brainstorming ideas into real-time projects and products. Technovaciòn'22 does not expect masterminds, but the students who are willing to upgrade our society and Environment. This event also enhances the networking amongst students within their native country and overseas.

As a token of appreciation, Winners and most ingenious participants will be provided with exciting prizes, Internship opportunities, Professional Mentorship guidance, etc. for further expanding their projects in the future. We encourage innovations from all corners of the world, thus building up a Technology-centered efficient society.Technovaciòn'22 is about to set a benchmark for Rural Development and Sustainable & Technological Development.

IEEE

IEEE stands for the Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers. IEEE is the world's biggest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

Sri Sairam Engineering College Computer Society Student Branch Chapter has been active since 2017. With the consistent efforts of passionate volunteers and Advisor Dr. Soma Prathiba, The branch has become highly vibrant as the years passed by.

Over the years 2020 & 2021, more than 70+ students along with Professionals have registered for the IEEE membership and engaged themselves in various volunteering activities at sectional, National as well as in Global levels.

Every year IEEE Day is celebrated lavishly on the 17th of February with lots of innovative events, National and International webinars for school and college students. Codefete, Step 2020, Technovacion’21 have been conducted by SSEC IEEE CS SBC with help of the SPAA organization. In 2021, The branch is still continuing its dedicated services to society and the Nation.
