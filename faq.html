FAQ

1. What is Technovacion'22?

Technovacion'22 is a 3-day Global Project Manifestation conducted by Sri Sairam Engineering College IEEE Computer Society -SBC in association with SSEC IEEE Educational Society and sponsored by IEEE SPAx. The motto of this event is Transforming ideas into Real time projects.

2.How is my registration confirmed?

After completing registration, you will receive a confirmation email with your registration information.

3.What is the maximum number of participants per team can have?

Technovaciòn'22 encourages you to have a maximum of three participants per team.

4.Does the event cost anything?

No, the event is free of cost.

5.How long will the Technovacion’22 challenge take?

The Challenge is broken down into 3 phases.
The first phase will be abstract selection.
The second phase will be Prototype presentation level 1.
Finally, on the last day of the challenge, you will be required to present the improved prototype from day 2.

6.Is the hackathon open to all?

Yes, it is open to all National and International participants

7.How does Technovacion'22 help student participants?

Technovacion’22 winners will be awarded a cash prize, IEEE branded Certificates and amazing gift vouchers. Apart from it, there are also many surprising elements yet to be declared on the event day.

8.What are the evaluation criteria for a presentation?

-Idea Implementation
-Technicalities
-Level of innovation
-Feasibility
-Market Adaptability

9.Who will pitch the ideas?

You and your team will pitch the ideas. It would be advantageous if you dealt with some preliminary research. If the project is impressive, you will be helped with the professional guidance.
