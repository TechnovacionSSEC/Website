This year’s Technovacion focuses on Rural Development by implementing technologies in the rural areas which creates a tremendous impact in the rural areas of the country. The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "Blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all" The SDGs were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by the year 2030.

“We have not inherited this earth from our forefathers; We have borrowed it from our children”.

By adhering to SDG goals framed by the UN General Assembly, Sri Sairam Engineering College aims to encourage students to conserve the environment and pass on the legacy of mother earth to the future generation. So let's bring innovative solutions toward rural development.

SDG-1
No Poverty

Eradicating poverty in all its forms remains one of the greatest challenges facing humanity.

SDG-3
Good Health and Well-Being

Health is wealth. Develop tech-based solutions to find and rectify the shortage of medicine, especially in rural areas.

SDG-4
Quality Education

Education is the passport to the future. Develop AI - Based solutions in the field of inclusive education.

SDG-6
Clean Water and Sanitation

Water Scarcity affects more than 40 percent of the people, an alarming figure that is projected to rise as temperatures do. So implement technologies to provide clean water and sanitation in rural areas.

SDG-7
Affordable and Clean Energy

Renewable energy is the winner when it comes to boosting the economy and creating jobs. So be creative and come up with good solutions for making use of renewable energy resources.

SDG-9
Industry, innovation and infrastructure

Develop solutions for the rural - urban technology gap by increasing access to resilient infrastructure, promote sustainable industrialization and foster innovations.

Open Innovation

Open to other Technologies implementing other SDG goals for the enhancement of RURAL DEVELOPMENT.
